How to Create a Pizza Crust from a Dough Ball

This soundless movie details how a pizza dough ball can be shaped out by hand. The crust is formed by flattening the chilled dough ball and turning at the same time in a clockwise motion. The flattened result is then placed on a screen to prepare for toppings.
