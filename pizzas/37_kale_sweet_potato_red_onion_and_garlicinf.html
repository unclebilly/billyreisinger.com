Kale, red onions, and sweet potatoes with garlic olive oil

Sauce

Garlic olive oil

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Red onions
  • Sweet potatoes

Description

We had some friends over for pizzas, and they brought some fresh kale from their garden. I didn't know what to do with it, so I pulled this recipe from Two Peas And Their Pod, which I found after searching for "kale pizza." It was definitely a hit! I ended up roasting some garlic cloves in olive oil, and using that oil to baste the pizza before putting the toppings on.
