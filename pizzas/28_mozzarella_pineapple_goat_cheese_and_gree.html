Mozzarella, pineapple, goat cheese, and green bell peppers with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Goat cheese
  • Green bell peppers
  • Mozzarella

Description

Yet another of the same. The crust came out pretty good on this one - the bottom was nice and crispy, and the crust was chewy and tasty in general. We used some of that generic mozzarella, which is really not that tasty - at least not as tasty as real, fresh mozzarella. Whatever, the pizza was still good.
