Cheddar cheese, fresh cilantro, ground turkey, mozzarella, roasted poblano peppers, and vine-ripened tomatoes with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Cheddar cheese
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Ground turkey
  • Mozzarella
  • Roasted poblano peppers
  • Vine-ripened tomatoes

Description

This pizza had a quarter section which was just pepperoni and cheese - for our young child. The other portion had a garlic butter sauce, in which I minced up about a teaspoon of cilantro stems. Then, I put a mix of cheddar and mozzarella cheese on it. I added some ground turkey, which I had pre-cooked and seasoned with cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. I also added some poblano peppers that I roasted, peeled, and diced. The last ingredient was a diced tomato.
