Mozzarella, chicken, green bell peppers, white onions, and whole wheat crust with barbecue sauce

Sauce

Barbecue sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Green bell peppers
  • Mozzarella
  • White onions
  • Whole wheat crust

Description

This was an amazingly delicious pizza. It was Jen's idea to use this topping combination. We used Sweet Baby Ray's barbecue sauce, which is our favorite. Then I seared a chicken breast on the stove until it was just barely cooked enough, cut it in thin slices, and slapped it on with thinly-sliced green bell pepper and thick-cut chunks of white onions. Mmmmm! I'm getting misty thinking about this one again. I've taken to mixing about half bread flour with half whole wheat flour - I feel like I'm getting nicer, stretchier dough with the higher gluten in the bread flour, but retaining the wonderful taste of the whole wheat.
