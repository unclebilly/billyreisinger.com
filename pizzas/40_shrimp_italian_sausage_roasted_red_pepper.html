Italian sausage, jumbo shrimp, kalamata olives, and roasted red peppers with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Jumbo shrimp
  • Kalamata olives
  • Roasted red peppers

Description

I love shrimp, but Jen and Evan do not - so I don't often get a chance to make a shrimp pizza. I had a pizza party with my son this night, though, so I busted out some jumbo pink shrimp on this pizza. The italian sausage was actually made of chicken, and it was mighty tasty.
