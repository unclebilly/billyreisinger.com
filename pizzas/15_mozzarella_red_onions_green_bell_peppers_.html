Mozzarella, red onions, green bell peppers, and pepperoni with marinara

Sauce

Marinara

Ingredients

  • Green bell peppers
  • Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Red onions

Description

Ah, it is not often I get to enjoy pepperoni on my pizza. This was a treat! We invited Jen's parents over for dinner, and that was my excuse to buy some nice pepperoni for this pizza. Needless to say, this was a classic pizza. Definitely good re-heated as well. I was glad that there were leftovers - hell yeah!
