Zucchini, roasted garlic, oregano, parsley, and whole wheat crust with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Oregano
  • Parsley
  • Roasted garlic
  • Whole wheat crust
  • Zucchini

Description

Amazing! First, I peeled, halved, and roasted garlic cloves from 2 bulbs in olive oil. Next, I put them on a pizza along with some fresh zucchini, parsley, and garlic butter. I thought that wheat crust complemented the toppings nicely!
