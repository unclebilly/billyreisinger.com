Heirloom tomatoes, green bell peppers, zucchini, pork and wild rice sausage, red onions, goat cheese, and brie with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Brie
  • Goat cheese
  • Green bell peppers
  • Heirloom tomatoes
  • Pork and wild rice sausage
  • Red onions
  • Zucchini

Description

We had some friends over for dinner, and we wanted to have a "top your own pizza" party. I got a bunch of ingredients ready, and prepared the pizza dough with garlic butter and cheese ahead of time - when our friends came, they brought some of their garden veggies for us to use, too. The result was 2 pizzas that had tons of different things on them. We had lots of fun, and the food was great!
