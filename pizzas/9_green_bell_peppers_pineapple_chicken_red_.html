Green bell peppers, pineapple, chicken, red onions, and goat cheese with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Goat cheese
  • Green bell peppers
  • Pineapple
  • Red onions

Description

This was a fairly decent pizza. It was a little on the greasy side. It is possible to put too much garlic butter on, and I think that's what happened with this one. Regardless, this is a fantastic topping combination. I remember the leftovers from this one being pretty good.
