Goat cheese, pepperoni, pineapple, and roasted poblano peppers with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Goat cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Pineapple
  • Roasted poblano peppers

Description

I made this pizza for Valentine's Day for my wife and son. One half had her favorite ingredients, and the other half had my son's favorite. I was happy eating either - I'm not picky when it comes to pizza. To make the heart shape, I shaped the dough into a circle (as if I was making a regular pizza) and used a pizza cutter to cut a heart shape out of the dough. Then, I put it on the screen and put toppings on it as I would with a normal, circular pizza.
