Pineapple, green bell peppers, goat cheese, and mozzarella with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Goat cheese
  • Green bell peppers
  • Mozzarella
  • Pineapple

Description

Another riff on Jennifer's favorite pizza topping combination. This one was cooked at 500 degrees for about 9 minutes. Flavor-wise, it was pretty good. I'm still looking for that extra umph in the dough, though - it was a little bland.
