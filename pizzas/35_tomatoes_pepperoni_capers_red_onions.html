Capers, pepperoni, red onions, and vine-ripened tomatoes with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Capers
  • Pepperoni
  • Red onions
  • Vine-ripened tomatoes

Description

This pizza was actually split into three sections: * Pepperoni * Pepperoni, capers, and red onions * Tomatoes, capers, and red onions This way, I was able to please all the family members at the table. That's the nice thing about pizza - you can make it to please everyone! I really like how the capers blend into this pizza, bringing out the flavor of the pepperoni and onions nicely. The crust on this one was airy and crisp on the bottom - just how I like it.
