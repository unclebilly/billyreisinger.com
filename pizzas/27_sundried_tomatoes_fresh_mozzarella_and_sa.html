Sun-dried tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and sauteed onions with rosemary garlic butter

Sauce

Rosemary garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Sauteed onions
  • Sun-dried tomatoes

Description

Actually, the sauteed onions were only on half of the pizza, as Jen doesn't like onions particularly. This was one of those "what do I have in the kitchen to make a pizza?" pizzas. We really didn't plan the ingredients. Surprisingly, the crust on this one was fabulous. Here's a tip: always rehydrate sun-dried tomatoes before putting them on your pizza. Soak them in hot water for ten minutes and then drain. Additionally, make sure you put them underneath any other toppings - unless you like the texture of leather on your pizza.
