Kalamata olives, vine-ripened tomatoes, goat cheese, and whole wheat crust with garlic butter

Sauce

Garlic butter

Ingredients

  • Goat cheese
  • Kalamata olives
  • Vine-ripened tomatoes
  • Whole wheat crust

Description

I rolled out the crust on this one with a rolling pin at the last moment before plopping it on the screen. I know it's cheating, but I wanted it just a bit thinner than I could safely get it with my hands - the wheat flour combined with the low amount of kneading on this one led to a dough ball that tended to rip. The end result was a delicious pizza - one of the best ones I have ever made.
