Fresh spinach, mozzarella, kalamata olives, and whole wheat crust with marinara

Sauce

Marinara

Ingredients

  • Fresh spinach
  • Kalamata olives
  • Mozzarella
  • Whole wheat crust

Description

After the last strange pizza, Jennifer and I were looking for something a little more down-to-earth. This pizza was a welcome change from the apricot weirdness from last time. We both love kalamata olives. I can't stress enough how much better they are than those plain old green or black olives you get in a can. But of course, they are a little pricey.
